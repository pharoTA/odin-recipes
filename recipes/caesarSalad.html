Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 4 cups day-old bread, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 6 anchovy fillets, mashed
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 head Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant but not browned, about 1 minute. Remove garlic and discard.
  2. Add bread cubes to skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until evenly browned, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together mashed anchovies, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and egg until well combined.
  4. In a large salad bowl, combine the lettuce and Parmesan cheese. Add dressing and toss to coat.
  5. Sprinkle with croutons and serve immediately.
